# 🎨 Brand Assets & Media Guidelines

The files contained in this directory (including logos, title cards, and official artwork) are protected under our **All Rights Reserved** policy. However, we grant limited permission for media use under the following strict guidelines:

---

## ✅ Allowed Uses
You are permitted to use these assets for:
* **Video Content:** Gameplay videos and reviews (within the video itself).
* **News & Articles:** Written articles, blog posts, or news coverage highlighting the mod.
* **Minor Modifications:** You may slightly adjust or resize these assets to fit your video layouts or articles, provided the modifications do not distort the branding and still accurately reflect the project.

---

## ❌ Prohibited Uses
* **No Video Thumbnails:** You are **strictly prohibited** from using these official assets in YouTube, TikTok, or other platform video thumbnails. This prevents viewers from confusing third-party videos with official project announcements.
* **No Re-branding:** You may not use these assets to brand your own mods, servers, or projects.

---

*Any use of these assets outside of the allowed guidelines without explicit written permission will be subject to a copyright takedown request.*
